About us

Rajalakshmi HealthCity is dedicated to providing advanced, compassionate medical care, especially to the underprivileged. Our expert team of healthcare professionals upholds the highest standards in treatment, education, and research.

About Rajalakshmi Health City

Rajalakshmi Health City is dedicated to advancing the knowledge and practice of medicine for the benefit of society. With a vision to establish a multispecialty hospital in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India, where in particular the poor could receive free advanced medical care in an atmosphere of love and compassion.

Here we provide the highest standards of medical treatment with the help of a dedicated team of Physicians, Nurses and other healthcare professionals. Our institution reaches far beyond the walls of our medical education to improve humanity and health throughout our community and the world, providing high-quality patient care and training the next generation of leaders in the medical field.



Our Mission

Medical Education

We are committed to creating globally competent doctors mentored by passionate faculty in exceptional infrastructure using the best teaching-learning tools available.

Patient Care

Our patients are treated with compassion and evidence-based medicine. We provide the highest quality healthcare at affordable prices to ensure finances are never a barrier.

Research

We attract the best researchers by creating ideal conditions for them to thrive and innovate in medical science.



Our Vision

To be a center of excellence in Medical Education, Research and Treatment by placing the needs of our students and patients ahead of all else.

Our Values

Compassion

We treat every individual with empathy and understanding.

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in all aspects of healthcare.

Integrity

We uphold ethical practices and transparency in all our work.

Innovation

We embrace new technologies and approaches to improve care.

Community

We are committed to serving our local and global community.

Accessibility

We ensure quality healthcare is available to all, regardless of economic status.
