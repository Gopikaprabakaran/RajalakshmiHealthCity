Our Leadership

Discover the transformative possibilities of the challenging, supportive RHC experience and see how our community shapes the world.

Dr. Haree Shankar Meganathan

I'm Dr. Haree S Meganathan, a radiologist by training and an educationist by passion. As the Vice Chairman of Rajalakshmi Institutions, I've had the privilege of helping build institutions that combine clinical depth, academic excellence, and long-term societal impact. Since 1997, Rajalakshmi Institutions has been committed to creating learning environments that empower students to lead with purpose. Rajalakshmi HealthCity carries this legacy forward into the realm of healthcare — where education, research, and compassionate care meet on one integrated campus. Our medical college and teaching hospital are anchored in a strong academic foundation, supported by modern infrastructure and faculty who are deeply invested in shaping tomorrow's healthcare professionals. Alongside this, we deliver accessible, patient-centered care through our general and super-specialty services, with a focus on dignity, empathy, and clinical excellence. From medicine, nursing and pharmacy to allied health sciences and medical research, every part of Rajalakshmi HealthCity is designed to work in harmony — serving people across all walks of life, and setting new benchmarks for healthcare delivery in India. We focus on raising the standard of what's possible in healthcare and education — through thoughtful design, values-driven leadership, and an unwavering commitment to quality in every detail. With extensive experience in medical education and healthcare administration, our Vice Chairman brings a wealth of knowledge and vision to our institution. Their leadership has been instrumental in shaping our institution's growth and development.

Dr. K. Vanitha

MBBS., MD (PAEDIATRICS)., DCH
Dr. K. Vanitha, Dean of Rajalakshmi Health City completed MD(Paediatrics) from Madras Medical College in 1994 and held various Academic and Administrative posts at Government Medical Colleges of Tamil Nadu for more than 20 years. Under National Health Mission, she looked after NICU activities and brought down the death rate of neonatal mortality at Coimbatore Medical and district level. She was awarded the Best Doctor award by the Government of Tamil Nadu in 2020 for COVID-19 activities. Currently she is associated with Rajalakshmi Health City for more than a year and actively managing the Medical College for its establishment.

Dr. Sukumaran Annamalai

MBBS., MD (GENERAL MEDICINE)., D.H.H.M
Dr. Sukumaran Annamalai, MD(Gen, Medicine), D.H.H.M is an alumni of Stanley Medical College and worked as Professor of General Medicine for more than 20 years at various Government Medical Colleges of Tamil Nadu. He also worked in the Medical Education Department of Government of Tamil Nadu for more than five years as Deputy/Joint Director. He also held the posts of Dean and Medical Superintendent for more than a decade in various private Medical Colleges. His good commanding skills and administrative skills as a Medical Superintendent helps Rajalakshmi Health City and hospital to develop as a renowned in the entire area.

Dr. Ushadevi Gopalan

MBBS, M.D, DGO, PhD
DR. Ushadevi Gopalan did her UG & PG from JIPMER Pondicherry and worked for more than 18 years in various Medical Colleges in Tamil nadu as UG & PG teacher. She has held the post of Head of the Department of Obstetrics & Gynecology and has trained many PG students and has been guide for their post Graduate Thesis. Her administrative skills, Research abilities, Clinical Acumen and teaching skills have made her an asset at Annaii Medical College and Hospital and helped to develop teaching and research in our College.
